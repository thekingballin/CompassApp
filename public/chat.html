    Chat Rules & Terms of Use

    1. Be respectful

    Treat all users with kindness, dignity, and respect.

    Discrimination or mistreatment based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or personal beliefs is not allowed.

    Avoid trolling or inappropriate behavior intended to provoke or disrupt others.

    Constructive discussion is welcome, but bullying, insults, threats, or personal attacks are not tolerated.

    2. No Illegal Content

    No drug discussions, sales, or promotion.

    No mentions weapons, violence, or threats.

    No sharing or discussing illegal activities.

    Hacking, doxxing, or any form of cybercrime.

    All chat logs are stored and may be reported to authorities if necessary.

    3. No Hate Speech

    Absolutely no racism, homophobia, transphobia, sexism, ableism, xenophobia or hate of any kind.

    Mild slurs are allowed but excessive use of foul or offensive language will not be tolerated.

    4. Keep It Clean

    No explicit or NSFW content, this includes but is not limited to:

    - Pornographic, sexually suggestive, or erotic material

    - Graphic violence or gore

    - Any content that is not suitable for minors

    This chat is meant to be safe and appropriate for all users.

    5. No Spamming or Flooding

    Don't send repetitive messages or walls of text.

    No advertising or self-promotion.

    Avoid all forms of disruptive formatting.

    6. Use an Appropriate Nickname

    Do not impersonate others.

    No offensive, misleading, or vulgar nicknames.

    You may be asked to change your nickname if it is found to violate these rules.

    6. Respect Personal Privacy

    Do not share personal info (yours or others): full names, addresses, phone numbers, passwords, etc.

    Do not ask others for private details.

    Final Provisions

    We reserve the right to modify or update these rules at any time. Continued use of the chat constitutes acceptance of the current version.

    All messages are logged. If you engage in illegal activity, your information may be shared with law enforcement. Use this chat responsibly.

    If you have any questions, concerns, or to report violations, please contact a moderator or admin within our Discord server.
